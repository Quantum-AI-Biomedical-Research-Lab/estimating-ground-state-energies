# estimating-ground-state-energies
Repository for the project 'Estimating molecular ground and excited state energies on quantum computers' executed at the Quantum AI Biomedical Research Lab, Department of Artificial Intelligence, Chang Gung University.

## Accompanying papers: 
1. Quantum circuit compilation for fermionic excitations using the Jordan-Wigner mapping. Renata Wong (2025). arxiv: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.07890](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.07890)
